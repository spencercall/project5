Favorite YouTube Channels

I love watching YouTube. It's what I love to do in my spare time. There are only a few channels I subscribe to, but the ones I do subscribe too are great. These are a few of my favorite channels that I recommend watching their videos.


MrBeast

MrBeast is one of most popular channels on YouTube and it's ran by Mr. Beast or others call him Jimmy. He makes video of all kinds of challenges and others things meant to give others money. I love the guy because he gives away most of the money he gained to others. This is the channel's most popular video and it's about Mr. Beast making a real live version of Squid Games.


Glitch

GLITCH is a indie animation studio/channel. They have made mutiple intresting animated shows on the channel. My favorite is Murder Drones and it's honestly one of my favorite shows on YouTube that I seen. This video is the pilot episode to start of the series.


America's Funniest Home Videos

America's Funniest Home Videos or AFV is a TV show that I watched since I was a kid and it has it's own YouTube channel. They show different kinds of funny videos and it had made me laugh multiple times. My Mom and I watched it's channel's videos sometimes.
